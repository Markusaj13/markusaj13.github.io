About

My name is Markus Johansson, I am 24 years old and I am currently studying UX-design at ‘Stockholms Tekniska Institut’. I am an extremely organized and detail-oriented person. I love it when the work I do makes a difference, be it for my team and friends or on a larger scale! Learning new programs and software is something I enjoy and is one of my many technological interests.

UI/UX Designer & Web Designer.

Prior to UX, I studied mechanical engineering at ‘Kungliga Tekniska Högskolan’ in Stockholm and I also voluntarily completed a year-long military service as Military police in which I got to study a couple of courses of law. My most recent and most relevant employment was at Coca-Cola European Partners as a Domestic/International Logistics coordinator. I learned a lot during my time there and I thoroughly enjoyed working with my fantastic colleagues and using my problem-solving skills in my day to day. My work impacted many people as I were responsible for overseeing the Coca-Cola distribution orders for major companies such as Ax-Food/Dagab and Martin&Servera as well as the entirety of Iceland.
